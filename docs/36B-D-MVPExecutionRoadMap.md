# 4. Execution Philosophy

## Primary Principle

Learning Speed over Technical Perfection

## Build vs Buy

Use existing farmer smartphones.

## Manual vs Automated

## Software vs Human Process

# 9. Event-First Strategy

ARI P0 is Event-First.

Not Form-First.

---

Instead of forcing structured forms,

ARI captures real-world events first.

Knowledge extraction occurs later.

---

## Knowledge Capture Principle

Capture First

↓

Structure Later

# 11. Event Types

P0 supports:

### General Daily Event

Default event type.

Used for any observation.

---

### Tree Event

Linked to a specific tree when available.

---

### Product Event

Capture agricultural products.

* Fertilizer
* Chemicals
* Tools

---

### Story Event

Capture:

* Farm History
* Experiences
* Lessons Learned
* Farm Stories

---

### Knowledge Event

Capture practical knowledge and techniques.

---

# 12. QR Policy

QR Scan is Optional.

---

If QR exists:

Event links to Tree Digital Twin.

---

If QR does not exist:

User can continue recording normally.

No adoption barrier should be created.

---

# 13. Offline-First Principle

Save ≠ Upload

---

When users press Save:

The event must be stored locally first.

---

Internet is not required.

---

Upload occurs later.

---

# 14. Upload Strategy

## Local First

Store all data on device first.

---

## Background Sync

Upload automatically when possible.

---

## Smart Upload

System manages:

* Compression
* Chunking
* Retry
* Resume

without user intervention.

---

## Upload Scheduling

Users may choose:

* Auto Upload
* WiFi Only
* Scheduled Upload
* Manual Upload

---

Example:

Daily upload at 20:00

with reminder and postponement options.

---
