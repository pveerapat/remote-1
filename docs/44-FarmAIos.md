# 1. Document Purpose

Farm AI Operating System (Farm AIOS) defines the conceptual operating system of ARI.

Farm AIOS is the orchestration and governance layer that coordinates:

* Humans
* AI Agents
* Digital Twins
* Evidence
* Knowledge
* Intelligence
* Trust
* Compliance
* Commerce
* Future Robotics

inside a unified agricultural intelligence platform.

Farm AIOS is a logical operating model and governance framework.

Farm AIOS is NOT:

* A mobile application
* A web application
* A robot operating system
* A replacement for ROS2
* A replacement for Linux

Farm AIOS exists above the ARI platform architecture and defines how the various layers of ARI operate together.

---

# 2. Mission

Farm AIOS exists to transform agricultural activities, observations, evidence, expert knowledge, AI capabilities, operational decisions, compliance requirements, trust mechanisms, and commercial outcomes into one coordinated operating model.

Farm AIOS supports the ARI mission of building:

* Digital Twin Layer
* Knowledge Layer
* Intelligence Layer
* Evidence Layer
* Trust Layer
* Commerce Layer

and provides the operational framework that connects them into real-world farm workflows.

Farm AIOS supports the ARI vision of creating the Intelligence and Trust Infrastructure for Global Agriculture.

---

# 3. Core Concept

Farm AIOS coordinates the full lifecycle of agricultural intelligence:

Observation
→ Evidence
→ Knowledge
→ Intelligence
→ Recommendation
→ Decision
→ Action
→ Outcome
→ Learning

The purpose is to ensure that observations collected in farms can eventually become reusable knowledge, trusted intelligence, better decisions, measurable outcomes, and continuous improvement.

# 5. Operating Model

Farm AIOS follows the operational flow:

Observation
→ Evidence
→ Knowledge
→ Intelligence
→ Recommendation
→ Decision
→ Action
→ Outcome
→ Learning

## Observation

Capture events, conditions, activities, and changes.

## Evidence

Record supporting proof and contextual information.

## Knowledge

Interpret observations using accumulated knowledge.

## Intelligence

Generate insights, forecasts, and recommendations.

## Recommendation

Provide suggested actions.

## Decision

Apply human governance and authorization.

## Action

Execute approved actions.

## Outcome

Measure results.

## Learning

Convert outcomes into future improvements.

---

# 6. Human-AI Collaboration Model

Farm AIOS is based on Human-AI Collaboration.

AI assists humans.

AI does not automatically replace human accountability.

---

## Human Responsibilities

Humans may:

* Observe
* Review
* Approve
* Execute
* Override
* Remain accountable

---

## AI Responsibilities

AI may:

* Analyze
* Detect patterns
* Retrieve knowledge
* Summarize information
* Generate recommendations
* Support decision making

AI recommendations do not automatically become actions.

---

## Human Review Requirements

Human review is required when:

* Confidence is low
* Evidence is incomplete
* Risk is significant
* Compliance may be affected
* Commercial impact is material
* Safety concerns exist
* Robotic authority boundaries are involved

---

## Escalation Requirements

Escalation may occur when:

* Evidence is contradictory
* Confidence is insufficient
* Human input conflicts with evidence
* Authority limits are exceeded

# 8. Authority Model Integration

Farm AIOS separates:

* Observation authority
* Recommendation authority
* Approval authority
* Execution authority
* Override authority
* Accountability

Observation does not imply approval authority.

Recommendation does not imply execution authority.

Execution does not remove accountability.

Human accountability remains central.

All robotic authority remains governed by Document #43.
