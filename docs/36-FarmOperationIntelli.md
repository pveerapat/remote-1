# Smallholder Farm Operation Model v1.0

---

# Strategic Context

Most agricultural platforms are designed for large farms.

ARI is different.

ARI is designed primarily for:

* Smallholder Farmers
* Family Farms
* Owner-Operator Farms
* Community-Based Farms

because these represent the majority of agricultural producers in Thailand and much of Southeast Asia.

ARI must adapt to the farmer.

The farmer should not need to adapt to ARI.

---

# Document Objective

Design the complete operational model of smallholder agriculture and define how ARI can become a practical daily operating system for smallholder farmers.

The objective is not to digitize farming for the sake of technology.

The objective is to:

* Increase farmer productivity
* Reduce risk
* Preserve knowledge
* Improve decision quality
* Improve income
* Reduce dependency on middlemen
* Prepare farmers for AI-assisted agriculture

while keeping systems simple enough for real-world adoption.
