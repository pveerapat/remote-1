# 2. Strategic Principle

ARI is not replacing farmers.

ARI augments human capability.

Humans remain responsible for:

* Judgment
* Approval
* Exception handling
* Experience-based decisions
* Field execution
* Knowledge creation

ARI provides:

* Observation
* Monitoring
* Prediction
* Recommendation
* Optimization
* Coordination
* Continuous learning

Goal:

Human + AI

must outperform

Human Alone

or

AI Alone.

# 3. Farm Operation Vision

## 3.1 Definition

Farm Operation Intelligence is the capability of ARI to transform farm operations from experience-only management into evidence-driven, continuously improving operations.

ARI views a farm as an integrated operational system consisting of:

* Goals
* Plans
* Resources
* People
* Equipment
* Tasks
* Evidence
* Results
* Knowledge
* Continuous Improvement

---

## 3.2 Operational Intelligence Loop

Farm Goal

→ Planning

→ Task Generation

→ Human / AI / Robot Assignment

→ Execution

→ Evidence Collection

→ Result Evaluation

→ Learning

→ Optimization

→ Better Planning

---

## 3.3 Core Purpose

ARI should help answer:

* What should be done today?
* Which task is most important?
* Who should do it?
* Which tasks are highest risk?
* Which tasks require expert review?
* Which tasks require AI assistance?
* Which future tasks may be automated?
* Was the work successful?
* What should improve next season?

# 9. Human-AI Collaboration Architecture

## Collaboration Modes

### AI Recommends → Human Approves

Used for:

* Treatment plans
* Harvest decisions
* Market allocation

---

### Human Reports → AI Validates

Used for:

* Observations
* Field reporting
* Evidence verification

---

### AI Detects → Human Investigates

Used for:

* Disease alerts
* Water anomalies
* Quality anomalies

---

### Human Overrides AI

Override allowed.

Reason and evidence should be recorded.

---

## Authority Boundaries

AI may:

* Recommend
* Prioritize
* Monitor
* Analyze

AI should not have final authority for:

* High-risk chemical use
* Major financial decisions
* Safety overrides
* High-risk robot missions
